Typical Iluvalar map
10 oil per player
Several defensive configurations (21 tile per oil ratio)
Semi symmetrical (your left and right neighbor are different, sweet for team setup as there is more than one variants: AABBCCDD or DAABBCCD) 
Feature: 
- Outer ring. Nice for ambush attacks.
- Free ring (no oil) toward the center. This allows strong players from both side to have a meeting point. And fight for the last 4 oil in the center.
